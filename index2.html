Instructions

1.Player positions the snake in the top-left corner of the game screen.

2.Player uses arrow keys to control the snake's direction.

3.As the snake moves forward, it leaves a trail behind, indicating the snake's length and position.

4.To increase the snake's length, the player causes the snake to eat an apple. Apples randomly appear on the game screen.

5.The game ends if the snake collides with the wall, another part of the snake, or itself.
